Motorbike Pollution

Pollution is the introduction of substances or energy that alter the environment or the quality of life of living beings. It can take various forms, including air, water, soil, acoustic, light, and thermal pollution, among others.

Consequences of motorbikes usage

  • Air pollution

    • Motorcycles, while providing mobility, exert environmental strain through emissions, resource consumption, and habitat disruption.

    Combustion engines emit pollutants, deteriorating air quality and contributing to climate change.

  • How do motorbikes construction affect

    • Manufacturing motorcycles demands substantial resources, fostering resource depletion and environmental degradation. Furthermore, the construction of motorcycle infrastructure often entails habitat destruction and fragmentation, endangering ecosystems and wildlife.
